Bio

2K12CR is the solo project of Ben Cloutier, a.k.a. Geordi La40. Following the release of “The Squat” in December of 2012, 2K12CR has been busting out sardonic hit after hit, never afraid to tackle a new topic or genre.

Although Geordi La40 is a specialist in Rap and R&B, he has written Rock, Metal, Emo, Country, Indie, and everything in-between. Many of those unforgettable tracks were assisted by the indomitable David Menendez, a.k.a Chad Stark from 2015-2022. Ask yourself, “What is the stupidest topic I could possibly write a song about”? 2K12CR has most certainly written a song about it. Whether you’re into women’s armpit hair, delusional redditors, manspreading, or scrapbooking, Geordi La40’s got you covered.
